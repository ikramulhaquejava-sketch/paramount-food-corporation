About Paramount Meat Corporation

Founded in 2025, Paramount Meat Corporation is a modern meat processing company located in Moradabad, India. Our facility is designed with advanced hygienic processing systems, cold storage infrastructure, and strict quality control standards.

We are committed to providing safe, fresh and premium meat products to domestic and international markets. Our vision is to become a globally trusted meat export brand through integrity, quality and excellence.

Our Modern Processing Facility

Our Moradabad-based factory follows strict hygiene and international food safety standards. Equipped with modern machinery and cold chain systems, we ensure freshness, quality and compliance with export regulations.

We maintain complete traceability, quality checks and professional packaging to guarantee customer satisfaction worldwide.
